# CEO Decision Brief Template (Operations)

Same structure as the CMO brief but with a People Impact Summary section — operational decisions affect people directly and the CEO needs to see that dimension.

---

```markdown
# Operations Decision Brief: [Question]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**From**: COO
**Recommendation**: [approve / approve-with-conditions / pilot-first / defer / reject]
**Confidence**: [1-10]/10
**Budget impact**: [Total cost, with delta from current if applicable]
**People impact**: [Net headcount change, roles affected]

---

## CEO Summary

[3-5 sentences. Full picture without reading the debate. Decision, why, what the CEO needs to approve (headcount, budget, org changes, timeline). Business language — cost, capability, risk, team health.]

---

## Recommendation Details

**Recommendation**: [approve / approve-with-conditions / pilot-first / defer / reject]

**Rationale**: [2-4 sentences referencing both sides]

**Operating model modifications**: [What changed from the original proposal]

**Action items**:
1. [Who] — [What] — by [When]
2. [Who] — [What] — by [When]
3. [Who] — [What] — by [When]

**Conditions / Gates**: [For approve-with-conditions or pilot-first]

**Risks accepted**:
- [Risk] — accepted because [reason]

**People impact summary**:
- Headcount: [net change]
- Roles affected: [which roles, how]
- Transition support: [training, change management, timeline]
- Attrition risk: [assessment and mitigation]

**Budget impact**:
- Current cost: [amount]
- Proposed cost: [amount]
- Transition cost: [one-time amount]
- Expected savings/ROI: [timeline and amount]

**Review date**: [YYYY-MM-DD] — [What to evaluate]

---

**Timeline**:

- Phase 2: [What] — [Date]

**Success metrics**: [Measurable outcomes with targets]
```
