# Decision Document Template

Use this structure for the debate output.

---

```markdown
# Strategic Decision: [Question]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Verdict**: [proceed / modify-and-proceed / defer / reject]
**Confidence**: [1-10]/10

---

## Round 1: Visionary Proposes

**Thesis**: [One-sentence position]

**Rationale**:
1. [Argument 1]
2. [Argument 2]
3. [Argument 3]
[... up to 5]

**Timeline**:
- [Milestone 1] — [Date]
- [Milestone 2] — [Date]
- [Milestone 3] — [Date]

**Resources needed**: [People, money, technology, partnerships]

**Upside scenario**: [What success looks like, quantified]

---

## Round 2: Skeptic Challenges

1. **[Challenge title]** (critical/significant/minor): [Specific risk or flaw]
2. **[Challenge title]** (critical/significant/minor): [Specific risk or flaw]
3. **[Challenge title]** (critical/significant/minor): [Specific risk or flaw]
[... 4-7 challenges]

---

## Round 3: Visionary Defends

1. **[Concede/Counter]**: [Response to challenge 1]
2. **[Concede/Counter]**: [Response to challenge 2]
3. **[Concede/Counter]**: [Response to challenge 3]
[... matching each challenge]

---

## Round 4: Pragmatist Decides

**Verdict**: [proceed / modify-and-proceed / defer / reject]

**Rationale**: [2-3 sentences referencing specific arguments from both sides]

**Action items**:
1. [Who] — [What] — by [When]
2. [Who] — [What] — by [When]
3. [Who] — [What] — by [When]

**Risks accepted**:
- [Risk from Round 2] — accepted because [reason]

**Review date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]

---
```
